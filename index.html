About me

Linus Agbleze is a researcher with a bachelor education in Geography and Resource Development from the University of Ghana as well as a Post-graduate certificate in Climate Change Management from the University of Edinburgh. Currently, he is a Master student at United Nations University and University of Bonn studying environmental risk and human security with research interests spanning but not limited to the areas of climate change adaptation and mitigation, vulnerability and disaster risk assessment and management. His works have largely focused on Africa.
