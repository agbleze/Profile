About me

I am a researcher with a masters degree in environmental risk and human security from United Nations University and University of Bonn. My research interests span but not limited to the areas of climate change adaptation and mitigation, vulnerability and disaster risk assessment and management. I employ several data science tools including geospatial analysis to gain hidden insight in data for relevant action. I love exploring creativity with programming tools trying bring imaginations to life.
